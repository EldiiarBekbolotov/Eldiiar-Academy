Community Guidelines

Last updated: {{ now.strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

1. Academic Integrity

Maintain high standards of academic integrity:

  • Complete assignments independently
  • Properly cite sources and references
  • Do not plagiarize or copy others' work

2. Discussion Guidelines

When participating in discussions:

  • Stay on topic and relevant to the course material
  • Share constructive feedback and insights
  • Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
  • Use appropriate language
  • Respect diverse perspectives and backgrounds
  • Avoid personal attacks or harassment

3. Content Standards

All content shared must:

  • Be appropriate for an educational environment
  • Not contain hate speech or discriminatory content
  • Not include spam or promotional material
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property rights

4. Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Temporary suspension of account
  • Permanent account termination
  • IP address ban
