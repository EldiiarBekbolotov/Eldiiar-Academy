Getting Started

How do I create an account?

After filling out the Sign Up form, you will receive a confirmation email to make sure it is you. Click on the link in the email to finish setting up your account.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Dashboard and click the Delete button. You will receive a confirmation popup, and after you click Delete Account there will be no going back.

Account & Profile

How do I update my profile?

Visit your dashboard to update your profile information, including your username and bio.

What are energy points?

Energy points are earned by completing lessons and participating in discussions.
